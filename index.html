About Br. Michael Mwandira

Br. Michael Mwandira is a Catholic theology student with a deep passion for explaining the Catholic faith clearly, faithfully, and pastorally. His journey in theological studies has equipped him with a profound understanding of Scripture, Church history, liturgy, and moral theology.

Through "Ask Brother a Question," Br. Michael aims to provide thoughtful, well-reasoned answers that help Catholics and seekers alike to grow in understanding and love for the faith. What is the Catholic understanding of Purgatory?

Purgatory is a state of purification for souls who die in God's grace and friendship, but are still imperfectly purified. They undergo purification to achieve the holiness necessary to enter the joy of heaven (Catechism of the Catholic Church, 1030-1032).

This teaching is supported by Scripture (2 Maccabees 12:46, 1 Corinthians 3:15) and has been part of Christian tradition since the early Church...
